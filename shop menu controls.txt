PC:
	Scroll Up: Smoke(default binded to Q)
	Scroll Down: Attack(Left Mouse Button)
	Select: Activate(default binded to F)
	Back/Exit: Melee(default binded to V or E)


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


PS3:
	Scroll Up: Smoke(default binded to L2)
	Scroll Down: Attack(default binded to R1)
	Select: Activate(default binded to [])
	Back/Exit: Melee(default binded to R3)
